A Smile For You To Return To

Lachlan Macleod, 2010

I’m supposed to be strong
To show you I’m fine
A smile
For you to return to
When
You’ve had enough time


And I’ll take it
I’ll do it
Convince myself not
To send unsubtle messages
Tears
Scratches


But part of me
Wants you
To know that it hurts
And it’s hard
Not to tell you
Everything


Just because
I don't know
How you feel
What you want
What you need
Me to do about
Anything


I just hope that you’re well
And that soon you’ll be back
That I’ll still be your friend
A smile
For you to return to


Sorry
