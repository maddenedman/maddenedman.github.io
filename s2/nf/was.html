Was

Lachlan Macleod, 2010

I miss
Your visage on my mind
Your existence in my synapses
Bus-stop
Feeling hopeful


I miss
Your dress in the pool
Your head on my shoulder
Motivation
Feeling content


I miss
Your hand in mine
Your cold fingers warmed
The edge of sleep
Feeling wanted


I miss
Your words in my ears
Your tears on my shoulder
You are significant, indispensable
Feeling needed


I miss
Your head on my chest
Your hand on my neck
Gentle scent of Sangria
Warmth


I miss
Your cheerful company
Your candid conversation
Speaking the unspoken
Feeling complete
