Sweet Release (Day 4)

Lachlan Macleod, 2010

I thought it would be never ending
But at last
Sweet release


She came in
While I was working
Business Attire
Wore it well...
A coy smile


Not so cute as to
Disarm
Or to slap me across the face
And make me
Giddy


She was subtle
The best kind of cute
It may have taken a few seconds
To even see it


Playful banter
Tricky Machine
Probably meant nothing
I didn't get her number
Or her name
I may never see her again
And it doesn't matter


She was a good distraction
A reminder of the outside world


She didn't even look a little bit sassy
And I doubt she enjoys jazz


But now I know
You're far from the only fish in the sea


Still love you though ;)
