Spotting Interstellar Movement

Lachlan Macleod, 2008

Spotting Interstellar Movement,
All at once,
Life makes sense
And shatters
Into disarray


You
Are the one I’d love
To be with
For at least a while


Utilising newfound strength
From inebriation, I,
Stuck in phony words,
Endeavor to discover
Your centre of imagination,
Your goals and fears


You,
Chime a C,
Of Major Key,
Pleasant on the ear
Fascinating me,
With intermittent B
Serenity to hear


Do past patterns,
Of doubt
Hold now?
I’m held down between
Past pessimism and
Future hope
